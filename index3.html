life

Life is the space in which the story of our life plays out. We can visualize it as being similar to a television screen in function. When we watch a movie, we become oblivious of our surroundings. Our Awareness moves into the story and the screen becomes invisible to us.

We remain unaware of the screen as long our attention remains rooted in the story. However, when we get bored with the story, our attention moves back to the screen and we become aware of our surroundings once again.

We realize that everything in our life is predestined, we lose interest in the story of life. Our attention then shifts from the story to the space in which this story plays out and we become aware of life. This space is called Life.

time

When our attention shifts to the space in which the story of our life plays out, time stops. The concept of time which includes past, present and future are relevant only within the story. The concept of time is meaningless in context of the tv screen.

When our awareness dwells in the space of life, Past and Future collapse into the present and we experience our timeless self. We realize that our life story is not actually our story at all. We just have a samll role in this story. This story is shared by everything that exists in the space of life.

the present

Past and Future exist only within the context of our Present.

They are both visualizations that happen in our field of perception and they happen in the Present. When we visualize past our future, we spend our time absorbed in the visualization instead of experiencing the present moment. Past and Future exist as parasites that eat away our present.

When all our attention returns to the present moment, the quality of our perception improves and we become aware of both the story as well as the space in which it plays out.

living life

This state of existence is very different from the one which we are used to. In this state the happenings of our life no longer generate emotions, thoughts, and feelings within us. There are no highs or lows, we feel the same way all time. We feel content, secure, happy, and peaceful all the time regardless of the circumstances of our life.

When our attention pulls out of the story, we start noticing things that we were unaware of before. We become more attentive and perceptive. Our senses sharpen up, colors appear brighter and the same food tastes a lot better. We start enjoying the experience of life.
