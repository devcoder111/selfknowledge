no fear of death

birth

We were born when the male sperm fused with the female egg to form the first cell of our body, the Zygote. The sperm and egg cells were individually alive before they fused. The resulting zygote was alive as well. The zygote then divided into the various cells of our body, each of which was individually alive.

Birth does not create Life, nor does Death destroy it. Life exists before we are born and will continue long after we die

No Fear of Death

death

Our awareness is made up of the collective awareness of the cells and microorganisms the constitute us, like the way on an image on the screen is made using pixels. After we die, we become inanimate and lose the ability to participate in the story of life, however, most of our cells are still individually alive and our awareness continues. Death does not kill our awareness.

Even while we are alive, the cells in our body continually die and are replaced with new cells made from the food we eat. Millions of cells die every second and millions more are born. Our awareness tunes our this large-scale birth and death that hapeens in our body every second. This Birth and Death of cells in our body continues even after we die.

the experience of death

Unable to participate in the story of life but aware of the happenings around and within us, the residual 'me' slowly distorts and fades away in agony as the cells of our body are replaced by microorganisms that first consume us and then each other. The residual 'me' is created by various cells and microorganisms that constitute us. It exists till the last of our cells get eaten away and the remaining microorganisms consume each other and die as well.

It is said that our awareness wonders this earth for 40 days after death before it dissolves. During this time, it has plenty of time to dwell on the events its life. The virtual 'me' dwells in regret about the things it should have done or not done while it was alive. The intensity of regret and misery depends on the kind of life we have lived.

After spending some time in the misery of hell, our awareness dissolves into the light of the One Eternal Conscious Life.

the judgement

It is said that when we die, we are judged by Saint Peter who scans our value system, which survives physical death, and looks at the residual footprint of our deeds that exists as value within us.

After Physical death, our Unfullfilled desires and aspiratinos come to the footprint and Negative feelings get amplified manifold. Those who are still attached to the story of their life are denied entry into heaven and remain attached to their rotting physical body and experience hell.

Those who know that the deeds of their life were predestined according to the will of God are not answerable to Saint Peter. They live in heaven while they are alive and after death.

Transcending death

Happy people are not disturbed by the happenings around them, in life or death. Their attention already dwells in the light of consciousness while they are alive, so after death, when the consciousness returns there, they don't even notice the difference. From their viewport, death does not exist.

Those who realize that their existence is temporary and illusionary, somewhat like a character in a movie detach from the identity of their character even while alive and easily after death.
